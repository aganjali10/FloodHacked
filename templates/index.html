BECAUSE WE CARE

We aim to develop an optimal solution for ensuring an organised distribution of resources during natural calamities such as the recent floods in Kerala.




We cannot stop natural disasters but we can arm ourselves with knowledge: so many lives wouldn’t have to be lost if there was enough disaster preparedness.

Petra Nemcova



“Flood relief” is a software providing an effective alternative to the existing ad hoc management scheme used by the government during floods. It provides a real time dynamic way of determining the minimum time and number of resources required to maximise the number of people rescued.
